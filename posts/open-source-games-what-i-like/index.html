Monmouthshire and Forest of Dean Freebooter

Open source games what I like

2 January 2020
  • Battle for Wesnoth: a turn based fantasy strategy game.
  • Chromium BSU: fast paced, arcade-style, top-scrolling space shooter.
  • Freedoom: free and open source FPS game based on the Doom engine.
  • Hedgewars: a turn-based strategy, artillery, action and comedy game (like Worms).
  • Minetest: free, open source voxel game engine and game, fun to build stuff in.
  • OpenArena: community-produced deathmatch FPS based on GPL idTech3 technology.
  • OpenMW: open source engine for The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind. Original game files required.
  • OpenTTD: Transport Tycoon Deluxe as imagined by FOSS.
  • Red Eclipse: a fun Arena shooter.
  • Shapez.io: factory line puzzle game, similar to Factorio.
  • Widelands: a Settlers-esq strategy open source strategy game.
  • Xonotic: free and fast arena shooter.
